Meet Our Maos

One of our missions at The Mao Lounge is to provide a home to cats that don't have one. This way, we are able to prevent cats from being euthanized, and give them a comfy space to live before they are taken to their forever home.
All cats are up for adoption, please ask one of our Cat Managers or email us at TheMaoLounge@gmail.com

tiki

Tiki was brought to us by a couple whose cat had given birth. All his littermates had been adopted except for him. The couple could not keep him, so they brought him here.

Age: 3 months

Favorite Toy: The Cat Managers' shoelaces

Best Friend: Sesame

Tiki is an energetic and mischevious little purrball. He tends to get the zoomies often, and loves to swat at anything dangly so protect your shoelaces!

But he's also the sweetest cuddlebug and can stay in your arms for hours. He's quite a talkative one so be prepared for a lot of tiny meows from him.

sesame

Sesame was born to one of our cats here at The Mao Lounge. His mom Winnie was brought in while she was pregnant with Sesame.

Age: 3 months

Favorite Toy: Tiki's tail

Best Friend: Tiki

Sesame is a chill little kitten that gets easily distracted. He loves to waddle around and greet new people. For a 3 month kitten, he is surprisingly social!

He is a bit squirmy at first, but once he warms up to you, he'll follow you every where.

cherry

One of our Cat Manager's found Cherry on the way to work. According to her, she stepped out of her house, and as she walked down her porch she heard a quiet meow from below the stairs. She took a peek and found Cherry curled up in a ball.

Age: 2 years

Favorite Toy: Cherry Cat Mobile

Best Friend: Mochi

Our Cherry is a bit of a diva. He never comes up to you, but will sit in his chosen spot and observe you until you come up and make a move. He'll reject your advances at first, but if you feed him, he'll let you pet him for a bit.

Don't expect him to stay there for long though. For him, enough is enough and he'll hop away. But he is such a sweet boy and will give you the occasional headbutt of affection.

mochi

Mochi is a siamese kitty that one of our baristas rescued. This sweet girl had been abandoned by her owners after they moved out of their home. She was found in a cardboard box underneath a bush.

Age: 1 1/2 years

Favorite Toy: Strawberry Plush

Best Friend: Cherry

Mochi the sweetest and chattiest cuddlebug. She loves to play and will follow you around if she likes you. She is super intelligent and can do a ton of tricks! To see them in action, ask a Cat Manager to demonstrate

She is super socialable and loves meeting new people.
