Relax With Some Kitties and Coffee

Welcome to The Mao Lounge — your cozy corner for coffee, cats, and comfort. Tucked away from the bustle of everyday life, our café is a warm, inviting space where you can sip your favorite brew surrounded by the soft purrs and playful antics of our resident kitties. Whether you're here to relax, recharge, or make a few furry friends, The Mao Lounge feels just like home — but with more whiskers.

Our Cat and Coffee Combo of the Month

Every month we choose a very special cat at our cat cafe to highlight! This furball gets a limited drink inspired by them. All proceeds from this particular drink will go straight to the kitty of the months. This means new toys, money for vet treatments amongst other things!

MEET MOCHI

Mochi is a siamese kitty that one of our baristas rescued. This sweet girl had been abandoned by her owners after they moved out of their home. She was found in a cardboard box underneath a bush.
favorite toy: her strawberry plush
personality: the sweetest cuddlebug. don't be surprised if she comes up to you and headbutts you!

Visitation Guidelines

We do not require booking prior to visits. The cats get to roam around the cafe portion under tight supervision from one of our Cat Managers

However, we follow a schedule and only allow 2-3 cats into the cafe area at a time .

The rest of the cats are kept in a seperate cat lounge. Access to the cat lounge is $3 a visit

Cat Interactions: Tips and Rules

  • Do not feed the cats any baked goods or drinks
  • If you want to feed them outside of the cat lounge, ask a Cat Manager for some cat treats
  • No aggressive behavior- this includes pulling ears and tails
  • Let them come to you! Cats are very social when its on their terms

violation of these rules can lead to being banned from the cafe, so interact with our cats responsibly!
